Frequently asked questions

How far in advance should I book?

For peak seasons we recommend 3–6 months. Off-peak trips can be arranged in as little as 2 weeks.

Are flights included in your packages?

International flights are optional add-ons; all in-destination transfers are always included.

Can trips be fully customized?

Absolutely — every itinerary is tailored around your dates, pace, and interests.

Do you support solo travellers?

Yes. Roughly a third of our travellers are solo — we curate safe, hosted experiences.
